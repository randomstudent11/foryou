"When you pass through the deep, stormy sea, you can count on me to be there with you.
When you pass through raging rivers, You will not drown.
When you walk through persecution like fiery flames, you will not be burned; the flames will not harm you"
Isaiah 43:2




I feel like sometimes we can all be looking at the same thing but SEEING something different. I took this photograph before going for a surf one day. Everyone else was focussed on the waves crashing onto the shore and didn't even notice the beauty going on before them. It almost appears divine, the way the light streams through the clouds like this. It's so easy to get caught up in things, especially with a fast pace worklife. Or sometimes when we are so absorbed in the negatives that life throws at us. We can neglect to take a minute just to see the beauty that is in our lives. Just like this photo, sometimes we can be surrounded by dark clouds but we should always remember that there is light on the other side.
